ABOUT

Alexa Holmina (She/Her) is a proud Filipino transgender woman passionate about helping other trans women discover their true selves and unleash their full potential. As a transformational coach, Alexa uses her lived experiences to create a safe and non-judgmental space for her clients to explore their gender identity and work towards their goals.Alexa has always been passionate about fashion and styling. Alexa believes beauty is more than skin deep; it starts with feeling confident and comfortable in your body. As a transformational coach and catalyst for empowerment, Alexa works with her clients to help them embrace their true selves and feel empowered to live authentically. Alexa is passionate about helping trans women achieve their dreams and unleash their full potential, and she is committed to creating a safe and supportive community for all.
