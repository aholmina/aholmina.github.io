SOCIAL

Social transition is when a person changes their name, pronouns, clothing, hairstyle, or other aspects of their appearance to reflect their gender identity.

MEDICAL
Medical transition is when a person undergoes hormone therapy or surgery to alter their physical characteristics.
LEGAL

Legal transition is when a person changes their official documents, such as birth certificate, passport, or driver’s license, to match their gender identity.
