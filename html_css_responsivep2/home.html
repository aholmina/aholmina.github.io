Social/Medical/Legal Information
SOCIAL

Social transition is when a person changes their name, pronouns, clothing, or other aspects of their appearance to reflect their gender identity.

MEDICAL

Medical transition is when a person undergoes hormone therapy or surgery to alter their physical characteristics.It is an individualized process.

LEGAL

Legal transition is when a person changes their official documents, such as birth certificate or driver’s license, to match their gender identity.
